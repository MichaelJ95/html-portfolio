The best Movies According to Michael

My top 3 Movies of all-time

The Conjuring

One of very few horror movies that kept me entertained for the entire movie. Not a lot of jump scares and I really enjoyed the whole movie.

John Wick

Nice action packed movie from start to finish. I was entertained the entire movie and cannot wait for the next one!

Fast and the Furious

I have alwasy liked racing and this movie just made me wish I could do the same. The Movie kept me entertained and I really liked the cast.
